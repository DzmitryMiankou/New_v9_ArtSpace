minimalist museum in Deli

A minimalist museum is a space that is designed to showcase art or historical artifacts in a clean, simple, and uncluttered manner. The focus is on the pieces themselves, with minimal distractions from the surroundings. The design of the museum is often modern and sleek, with neutral colors and minimalist furnishings. The goal is to create a serene and contemplative atmosphere that allows visitors to fully appreciate the artwork or objects on display.
